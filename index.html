Helping Dogs Through Fees

The Dog Project is a revolutionary crypto project with a heart. Every transaction fee collected in our developer wallet will be donated directly to established dog charities around the world.

We believe that the power of blockchain technology can create positive change for our four-legged friends. From rescue operations to medical care, from finding forever homes to providing food and shelter - your participation in our ecosystem will directly contribute to making dogs' lives better.

Transparency First

All donations will be tracked and publicly verifiable on the blockchain. We will provide monthly reports showing exactly where every dollar goes.

Our Partner Dog Charities

We work with established, reputable organizations that share our mission to help dogs in need.

ASPCA

The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals provides medical care, rescue services, and adoption programs nationwide.

Best Friends Animal Society

Leading the movement to end the killing of dogs and cats in America's shelters through adoption programs and spay/neuter initiatives.

Canine Companions for Independence

Provides expertly trained assistance dogs to children, adults and veterans with disabilities, free of charge.

RedRover

Brings animals from crisis to care through emergency sheltering, disaster relief services, and financial assistance.

Petfinder Foundation

Supports animal shelters and rescue groups with grants, resources, and technology to help pets find homes.

Brown Dog Foundation

Provides funding for emergency veterinary care for pets whose families cannot afford treatment.

K9s For Warriors

Provides service dogs to military veterans suffering from PTSD, traumatic brain injury, and other trauma.

Greyhound Pets of America

Dedicated to finding loving homes for retired racing greyhounds and promoting the breed's welfare.

Animal Medical Center

Provides advanced veterinary care and sponsors programs for low-income pet owners in need of medical assistance.

International Fund for Animal Welfare

Works globally to rescue and protect animals, including disaster response and wildlife conservation.

Humane Society of the United States

The nation's largest animal protection organization, fighting for all animals through advocacy, education, and hands-on care.

American Humane

The country's first national humane organization, dedicated to ensuring the safety, welfare and well-being of animals.
